About Us
 

Our History

 

Home Vet Finder is the fastest and easiest way to connect with top veterinarians in the Long Island, New York vicinity. Home Vet Finder will help you find the care you need, in the comfort of your home. Once you select the veterinarian you would like to see, we will help you set up your first appointment.

 

Our Mission

 

Our pets are our family, and getting them the best healthcare is important. When that means finding a veterinarian that makes home visits, we’ve got you covered! We find the best veterinarians in your area will work with you in the comfort of your own home, at a time that works best for you.
